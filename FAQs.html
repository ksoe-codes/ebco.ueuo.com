FAQ / Commonly Answered

How do I Sell on EBCo
  • –
    Sign in with Google account provided by the organisation
  • –
    Choose the Seller page 
  • –
    Add our item, description with images and submit
  • –
    wait for a confirmation email from the team
  • –
    Hooray! your product is now on the buyer's page :) 
Where do these items come from?
  • –
    These items are nothing but the items which sellers around you want to sell their used goods
  • –
    once you are interested to buy a thing displayed on the buyer's page, 
  • –
    you will be under watch list of the seller.
  • –
    the seller next deciedes whether he wants to sell the item to you or someone else in the watchlist
  • –
    once the seller approves you on the watchlist, you will get a notification through email/sms and the sellers information
  • –
    you can further contact the seller and get your product from him.
How do I get the items to me
  • –
    The items you choose to buy among your organization, dosen't depend on us (EBCo)
  • –
    Once you are approved by the seller after the Watchlist,
  • –
    You get the contact details of the seller
  • –
    You contact him on your own & get the deal done yourself as both the buyer and seller are from the same organization, Delivery isn't really an issue.
  • –
    EBCo dosen't encourage any sort of Delivery services.
On What Factor does the Price of the item Depend?
  • –
    The price of any item, is solely fixed by the Seller himself
  • –
    Once you are interested, click to get on the Watchlist
  • –
    If approved by the seller, you get to Contact the seller and have a good deal.
  • –
    Any Cost Displayed by the seller, 2% from the seller and 3% from the buyer is to be given to EBCo. 
What if two People are selected form the Watchlist
  • –
    If Two People are slected from the watchlist by the seller
  • –
    Both the people are contacted by the seller
  • –
    An Auction is to be held by EBCo
  • –
    The one who wins, takes the price :)
